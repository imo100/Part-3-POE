About Us

Charles Greenway is an expert garden and plant care company whose main objective is to offer the best possible landscaping services. The company was founded with a love for nature and sustainable practices, offering the best and most reliable services.

Our History

Charles Greenway started as a small local business with the goal of helping homeowners maintain their gardens. Over time, it has grown into a trusted service provider known for its attention to detail and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide professional gardening services that promote sustainability, enhance natural beauty, and create clean, healthy environments for our clients.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading gardening service known for quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction while contributing to greener communities.

Our Team

Our team is made up of hardworking people who enjoy gardening and landscaping. Each team member is trained to do their best and make sure every job is done properly.
